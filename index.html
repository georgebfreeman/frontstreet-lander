FrontStreet: Empowering Boston's Black Artists

FrontStreet is a groundbreaking platform designed to elevate Black artists in Boston. We're tackling systemic inequities in the art world head-on. Our mission: to amplify voices, showcase talent, and drive meaningful change in the arts community.

Key Features: Amplifying Artistic Voices

Artist Profiles

Customizable spaces for artists to showcase their work and connect with collectors. Bios and portfolios available.

Discovery Feed

AI-powered recommendations introduce users to new talent. Our algorithm ensures fair exposure for all artists on the platform.

Event Listings

Comprehensive calendar of exhibitions, openings, and workshops. Users stay connected to Boston's vibrant Black art scene.

Collector Tools

Seamless browsing and purchasing of available art. Collectors can easily inquire about purchasing from artists directly.
